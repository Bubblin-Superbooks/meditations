  2. You are presently to die, and yet you have not attained to simplicity or calm, or to disbelief that you can be hurt by things external. You have not learned to be kindly to all men, or to count just dealing the whole of wisdom.
  3. Scan closely that which governs men; see what are their cares, and what they pursue or shun.
