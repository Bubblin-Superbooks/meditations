  2. The guiding mind knows what its own condition is, how, and upon what matter its work is done.
  3. The best revenge is not to copy him that wronged you.
  4. Find your sole delight and recreation in proceeding from one unselfish action to another, with God ever in mind.
  5. The ruling part of you is that which rouses and steers itself, making itself what it wishes to be, and making all that happens take such appearance as it will.
  6. All things are accomplished according to the will of universal nature. There is no other nature to influence them which either comprehends the former from without, or is contained within it, or exists externally, and independent of it.
