That is the best to which you can attain: and failure in that is the only proper miscarriage. He who in everything follows reason is always at leisure, yet ever ready for action, always cheerful, yet composed.
  2. As soon as you awake ask yourself: Will it be of consequence to you if what is just and good be done by some other man? It will not.
