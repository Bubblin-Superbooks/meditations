1. To the stone thrown up it is no evil to fall; no good to rise.
  2. Penetrate the souls of men, and you will see what judges you fear, and how they sit in judgment on themselves.
  3. All things are in change. You yourself are under continual transmutation, and, in some sort, corruption. So is the whole universe.
  4. Another's sin you must leave with himself.
  5. The ceasing of any action, the extinction of any keen desire, or of any opinion, is as it were a death to them. This is no evil. Think again of the ages of your life; childhood, youth, manhood, old age. Each change of these was a death. Is there anything to dread here?
